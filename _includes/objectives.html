Objectives

  • To make an efficient, autonomous machine that replaces the job of a courier boy in a building.
  • The delivery robot should be able to gain information about the environment.
  • Physical autonomy - the robot should be able to take care of itself.
  • Exteroception - it delivery bot should be equipped with a number of functional sensors required for the task.
  • Autonomous optimal navigation.
  • End to end safe delivery of product.
