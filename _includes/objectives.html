Objectives

  • To make an efficient, autonomous machine that replaces the job of a courier boy in large campuses.
  • The delivery robot should be able to gain information about the environment.
  • Physical autonomy - the robot should be able to take care of itself.
  • Exteroception - the delivery bot should be able to make use of the attached functional sensors to improve it's performance in a task.
  • Autonomous optimal navigation from the locker region to the co-ordinates of the person and back.
  • End to end safe delivery of product.
