Objectives

  • To make an efficient, autonomous machine that replaces the job of a delivery person in multistorey buildings.
  • A motorised drive arrangement.
  • Like driverless cars, the delivery robots use cameras, GPS and radar to "see" their urban environment and navigate through it.
  • A sensor module for generating signals to control the machine.
  • A controller for commanding the arrangement.
  • End to end safe delivery of product.
